Alabama Emerging as the Favorite to Land Five-Star Wing Caleb Holt

By Mason Whitaker | Senior Recruiting Analyst
Updated: March 3, 2026

The recruitment of five-star wing Caleb Holt is entering its decisive phase, and momentum appears to be building behind Alabama as the frontrunner.

While multiple national programs remain in contention, sources indicate the Crimson Tide have positioned themselves strongly heading into the final stretch of Holt’s decision timeline.

Arizona: The Main Competition

Arizona is widely viewed as Alabama’s most significant challenger. Much of that stems from Holt’s established relationship with Wildcats head coach Tommy Lloyd. The connection between Holt and Lloyd has been consistent throughout the recruiting process, giving Arizona legitimate staying power in this battle.

Lloyd’s developmental approach and offensive structure have resonated with Holt, making the Wildcats the primary program Alabama must overcome.

Houston Lurking as a Dark Horse

Houston remains firmly in the picture as a dark horse candidate. The Cougars’ culture, defensive identity, and national competitiveness have quietly made an impression.

Though not considered the frontrunner, Houston’s disciplined system and proven postseason success could generate late momentum.

Why Alabama Has the Edge

  • NBA-style pace and spacing system
  • Immediate opportunity for impact minutes
  • Strong SEC exposure and national visibility
  • Recruiting momentum building

With Arizona representing the strongest competition and Houston positioned as a potential spoiler, Alabama appears to hold slight momentum entering the final stages of Holt’s recruitment.
